About Us

Every year, it is estimated that 440,000 Americans die and millions of patients are harmed by preventable medical errors, which result in direct medical costs of over $17 billion. Baton’s mission is to improve patient safety and reduce preventable medical errors by increasing communication among the care team.

Baton solves three major problems that care teams face: it allows care providers to share information that is not captured by the electronic medical record, it informs the care teams of important updates in real time, and it interfaces with the EMR to ensure essential information makes it to the patient’s record.
